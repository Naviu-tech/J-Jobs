## Job Opportunity at Staley Technologies

### Position Overview
**Role:** Data Center Technician  
**Location:** Atherton, CA  
**Experience Required:** 3-5 years  

### Candidate Profile
Applicants should have substantial experience in data center environments, with a strong understanding of power and cooling systems. Proficiency in using cable testers and a solid grasp of network configurations are essential. The role requires meticulous attention to documentation protocols.

### Key Responsibilities
- Installation and maintenance of server racks
- Routing and securing high-density cabling
- Setting up Power Distribution Units (PDUs)
- Conducting cable testing with Fluke tools
- Documenting network layouts and connections
- Ensuring adherence to data center cleanliness standards

### Compensation and Perks
- Competitive hourly wage: $25.52-$33.44, based on experience
- Generous paid time off and holiday schedule
- Opportunities for career advancement
- Access to ongoing training and certifications

Join Staley Technologies in Atherton, CA, and contribute to our data center excellence.
