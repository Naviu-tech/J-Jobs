## Journeyman Electrician Position Overview

Seeking a skilled Journeyman Electrician for Bright Move Energy in Yorba Linda, CA. You will play a critical role in our long-term projects, focusing on the installation and maintenance of electric vehicle (EV) charging stations. Your expertise will ensure seamless electrical system integrations, while contributing to sustainable energy solutions.

## Qualifications for Journeyman Electrician

- 3-5 years of journeyman experience with an active California DIR certification.
- Proficient in NEC and California Electric Code, especially in EV charging station setups.
- Skilled in conduit bending, using multimeters, and interpreting blueprints.

## Responsibilities for Journeyman Electrician

- Install and troubleshoot electrical systems for EV charging stations.
- Accurately bend conduits and ensure precise alignment during installations.
- Conduct load calculations and install main service equipment for EV infrastructure.
- Collaborate with project managers and supervise apprentices, ensuring safety and quality.

## What We Offer Our Journeyman Electricians

- Competitive hourly rate: $41.01-$49.48, based on experience.
- Opportunities for career growth and advancement.
- Access to ongoing training and certifications.
