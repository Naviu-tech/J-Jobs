## About Our Journeyman Electrician Team

At IES Electric, our Journeyman Electricians are the backbone of our operations, renowned for their expertise and dedication to excellence. Based in Carlsbad, CA, we have established a sterling reputation for delivering top-tier electrical solutions across a variety of projects. Our team takes pride in working on complex commercial renewable energy installations, from solar arrays to advanced battery storage systems. While our primary operations are centered in Carlsbad, our influence extends to surrounding cities, including Oceanside, Vista, San Marcos, and Encinitas. We are committed to fostering a collaborative and dynamic work environment where innovation thrives, and safety is paramount.

## The Journeyman Electrician Position

### Day-to-Day Responsibilities

As a Journeyman Electrician at IES Electric, your day-to-day tasks will involve a mix of installation, maintenance, and troubleshooting activities. You will be responsible for installing high-voltage DC and AC systems, ensuring compliance with the latest codes and safety standards. Your role will also involve conducting system testing and commissioning for solar arrays to ensure they achieve optimal energy output.

### Types of Projects and Environments

Our projects vary widely, from large-scale commercial solar panel installations to cutting-edge energy storage solutions. You will work in diverse environments, including office buildings, industrial sites, and renewable energy farms. Each project presents unique challenges and opportunities for professional growth and skill enhancement.

### Team Structure and Supervision

You will be part of a skilled team of electricians and apprentices, working under the guidance of experienced project managers and engineers. Collaboration is key, and you will often work closely with these professionals to design and implement energy-efficient solutions.

### Growth Potential within IES Electric

We value career development at IES Electric, offering numerous opportunities for growth. As you gain experience and expertise, you can advance to senior positions, take on more complex projects, and even lead teams of your own.

## Core Journeyman Electrician Responsibilities

- **Renewable Energy Systems:** Install, maintain, and troubleshoot commercial renewable energy systems, including solar panels, energy storage systems, and inverters.
- **Conduit and Wiring Installation:** Route and install conduit (EMT, rigid) and wiring for high-voltage DC and AC systems, ensuring precision and compliance with NEC standards.
- **System Testing and Commissioning:** Perform comprehensive testing and commissioning for solar arrays, ensuring optimal energy output and adherence to local regulations.
- **Collaboration and Design:** Work closely with engineers and project managers to design and implement energy-efficient electrical solutions tailored to project specifications.
- **Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS):** Install and maintain BESS, integrating them seamlessly with existing building power systems.
- **Preventive Maintenance:** Conduct routine preventive maintenance on renewable energy equipment, including replacing faulty components and ensuring proper grounding.
- **Supervision and Safety:** Supervise apprentices, enforcing strict adherence to safety protocols and proper installation techniques.
- **Advanced Technical Duties:** Engage in advanced troubleshooting using the latest diagnostic tools, design and implement custom control systems for renewable projects, and participate in the development of new energy solutions tailored to regional needs.
- **Safety Protocols:** Adhere to rigorous safety protocols, including the use of personal protective equipment and compliance with OSHA regulations.

## Required Experience & Skills

- **Certification:** Must possess a valid California Journeyman Certification from the Department of Industrial Relations.
- **Knowledge Base:** Extensive knowledge of NEC and renewable energy codes, including specific regulations for solar and battery storage systems.
- **Experience:** Minimum of 4+ years as a Journeyman Electrician, with a focus on solar panel installations, inverters, and high-voltage DC systems.
- **Technical Proficiency:** Proficiency with multimeters, megohmmeters, and other diagnostic tools is essential.
- **Blueprint Interpretation:** Ability to read and interpret complex blueprints, electrical schematics, and energy system diagrams.
- **Problem-Solving Skills:** Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail are crucial for troubleshooting and optimizing system performance.

## Tools & Equipment

- **Daily Tools:** Journeyman Electricians at IES Electric use a variety of tools daily, including wire strippers, conduit benders, and crimping tools.
- **Personal Tools Required:** Electricians are expected to have a personal set of hand tools, including screwdrivers, pliers, and wrenches.
- **Company-Provided Equipment:** We provide specialized equipment such as power drills, multimeters, and safety gear to ensure efficient and safe operations.

## Physical Requirements

- **Lifting Requirements:** Journeyman Electricians must be capable of lifting up to 50 pounds regularly.
- **Working Conditions:** Work environments are varied, ranging from outdoor installations to indoor system integration, often requiring extended periods of standing or working at heights.
- **Safety Gear:** Required safety gear includes hard hats, safety glasses, gloves, and steel-toed boots.

## Training & Development

- **Mentorship Program:** We offer a robust mentorship program where experienced electricians guide newcomers through the nuances of the trade.
- **Certifications Available:** Opportunities to earn additional certifications, such as NABCEP Solar PV Installer Certification, are available.
- **Career Advancement Path:** Clear pathways for career advancement are established, allowing electricians to progress to senior roles and management positions.
